The Master's Journey

Julian Vane is an award-winning composer and multi-instrumentalist who has spent two decades at the intersection of classical precision and modern electronic experimentation. His work has been featured in major motion pictures and performed by some of the world's leading orchestras.

As a lead instructor at Harmony LMS, Julian focuses on the "Analog Soul" of music production—teaching students how to breathe life into digital workstations using organic textures and rhythmic complexity. His credentials include a Doctorate in Composition from the Juilliard School and three "Producer of the Year" accolades.
