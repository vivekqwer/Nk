Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later? expand_more

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you upgrade, the change happens immediately with a prorated charge.

What does '7-day trial' mean for the Starter plan? expand_more

It's 100% free for 7 days. You'll get access to core beginner modules. If you don't cancel before the 7th day, you won't be charged anything, but your access will simply expire.

Do the certificates carry weight in the industry? expand_more

Our certificates are co-signed by the Analog Master faculty. While they aren't university degrees, they are highly respected by production houses and labels as a sign of verified technical skill.

How do 1-on-1 sessions work? expand_more

In the Master plan, you get two credits per month to book a 30-minute private video call with a senior mentor for track review or technical troubleshooting.

Is there a student discount available? expand_more

Yes! If you are a student at a recognized institution, contact our support team with your ID to receive a 25% discount code for the Pro annual plan.

Can I download videos for offline viewing? expand_more

Offline viewing is currently available through our mobile app for Pro and Master subscribers. Desktop downloads are restricted for copyright protection.
